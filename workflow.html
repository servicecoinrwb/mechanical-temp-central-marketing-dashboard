Quarterly Compliance & Tax Routine

Due: April 15, June 15, Sept 15, Jan 15

Reference Guide

The "Who Does What" Split

  • Payroll Company: Handles Employees (SS, Medicare, Withholding, Unemployment).
  • You (Bookkeeper Pro): Handle Owner Income Tax (Profit) & Sales Tax.

Your 3-Step Quarterly Routine

1

The "Employee" Step (5 Mins)

Log in to Payroll Portal. Download "Quarterly Package" (Form 941 & State Unemployment). Save to "2025 Tax Documents" folder.

2

The "Owner" Step (15 Mins)

Open Bookkeeper Pro > Taxes Tab > "Quarterly Payment Estimates".

Go to IRS Direct Pay. Select "Estimated Tax (1040-ES)". Pay the amount shown.

3

The "Sales Tax" Step

Open Bookkeeper Pro > Reports > P&L > Look at "Income". Log into State Treasury, enter Total Sales, and pay the calculated tax.

How to Maintain Accuracy

To ensure Step 2 is accurate, perform this routine every Friday:

Action

Download CSV from Bank -> Drag into Bookkeeper Pro.

Categorization Cheat Sheet
  • Employee Paychecks "Paycheck" (Expense)
  • Payroll Fees "Professional Fees"
  • Parts/Fuel Normal Expenses
  • Money to You "Owner's Draw" (Equity)
  • Loan Payments "Transfer"
