Every Quarter

The Quarterly Routine

The "Who Does What" Split

  • Payroll Company: Handles Employee Taxes (SS, Medicare, Unemployment).
  • You (Bookkeeper Pro): Handle Owner Income Tax & Sales Tax.
1

The "Employee" Step (5 Mins)

Log in to Payroll Portal. Download "Quarterly Package" (Form 941 & State Unemployment). Save to "2025 Tax Documents".

2

The "Owner" Step (15 Mins)

Open Bookkeeper Pro > Taxes Tab > "Quarterly Payment Estimates". Go to IRS Direct Pay (Select 1040-ES) and pay that amount.

3

The "Sales Tax" Step

Open Bookkeeper Pro > P&L Report > Look at "Income". Log into State Treasury, enter Total Sales, pay tax.

Weekly Maintenance (Fridays)

Download Bank CSV -> Drag into App -> Categorize:

  • Employee Paychecks "Paycheck" (Expense)
  • Payroll Fees "Professional Fees"
  • Parts/Fuel Normal Expenses
  • Money to You "Owner's Draw" (Equity)
  • Loan Payments "Transfer"

Annual Filing Routine (Form 1040)

1. The "Payroll Packet"

Source: Gusto/ADP

  • W-3 Form: Summary of wages paid.
  • Form 940: Annual Federal Unemployment return.

*Just download these. Payroll company files them.

2. The "Profit Packet"

Source: Bookkeeper Pro

  • Profit & Loss Report: (Jan 1 - Dec 31).

*Go to Reports -> Profit & Loss -> Print/Save PDF.

How to File (The Actual Steps for Schedule C)

Step 1
Fill Schedule C

Enter "Total Income" from P&L. Enter "Payroll Expenses" (Match W-3). Copy other expenses from P&L.

Step 2
Calc Total Tax

IRS calculates tax on Net Profit.
Ex: Tax Bill = $30,000

Step 3
Subtract Pre-Payments

Subtract the checks you sent quarterly.
Ex: Paid $28,000 already.

Step 4
Pay Difference

Write one final check.
Ex: $30k - $28k = Pay $2,000.
